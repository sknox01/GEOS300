Glass dome of pyranometer [1]




<--Previous  Up  Next-->

The double glass dome allows transmission of short-wave irradiance which is then absorbed by the black absorber disk in the centre. The double dome prevents long-wave radiation from the sky to enter the system. The double dome is preventing conduction and the long-wave emission of the inner dome is the same as the long-wave emission of the instrument, so long-wave radiation does not heat the absorber.
